Return Policy

Your Satisfaction is Our Priority

‚è∞ Return Window

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for eligible items. Please ensure items are in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact.

Eligible Items for Return

The following party decoration items can be returned if they meet our return conditions:

  • Unused and unopened balloon packages
  • Decorative banners and signs in original packaging
  • Tableware sets (plates, cups, napkins) - sealed packages only
  • Party props and photo booth accessories - unused condition
  • Lighting decorations with original packaging and tags

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Inflated or used balloons
  • Opened packages of disposable tableware
  • Personalized or custom-made decorations
  • Perishable items like fresh flowers or edible decorations
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or purchased during clearance
  • Confetti, streamers, or other single-use items once opened

Return Conditions

To ensure a smooth return process, please make sure:

  • Items are in original, unused condition
  • All packaging, tags, and labels are intact
  • Original invoice or receipt is provided
  • Items are securely packaged for return shipping
  • Return request is initiated within 7 days of delivery

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive damaged or defective party decoration items, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.

Important: Please take photos of the damaged items and packaging to help us process your claim quickly.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned items:

  • Approved returns will be refunded within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable (except for damaged/defective items)
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed

How to Initiate a Return

Follow these simple steps to return your party decoration items:

  • Contact our customer service team via phone or email
  • Provide your order number and reason for return
  • Receive return authorization and shipping instructions
  • Pack items securely with all original packaging
  • Ship the items to the provided return address
